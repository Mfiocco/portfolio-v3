Flavors That Vibe

A Branding and website design that provides flavors for your ears and tastebuds.

VISUAL IDENTITY & WEB INTERFACE DESIGN

The Challenge

The primary goal was to create an engaging multipage website for a fictional company, Groov Donuts. The website would showcase the company’s current menu, selection of vinyl and merchandise while providing the public with easy access to important store information to drive customers to the physical location.

The Approach

To design a brand as enjoyable as the products it sells, Groov Donuts embraces playful colors, dynamic visuals, and bold typography, immersing all ages in its vibrant world of flavors.
